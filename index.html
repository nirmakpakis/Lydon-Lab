Get Involved

Come be a part of Lydon Lab!

  • Undergraduate Research

    Each year the Lydon Lab invites students enrolled in an undergraduate thesis course at McGill to apply to the lab.

  • Research Assistant

    The Lydon Lab hires RAs through the Work Study program on campus, usually in the early fall each school year.

  • Volunteer

    Apply to be a Volunteer in the Lydon Lab. Volunteering is a great way to get research experience.

  • Summer Research Fellowships

    The Lydon lab accepts applications for Summer Research Fellowships during the first months of the Winter term.
